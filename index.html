Why Eat Ayur Vedic?

The Ayurvedic way of preparing food supports good digestion and therefore good health. when spices are prepared in oil in a specific order the miture of oil and spices will draw out the proteins from the food so that they can be easily absorbed by the body. This means you will need less food to feel satisfied. And you will experience a better sense of well being.
